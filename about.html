Kong Ascension is an all new choose your own adventure kaiju fighting game! Ghidorah has just defeated Godzilla and has taken his place as King Of The Monsters! You will play as Kong, the alpha titan that will not submit to Ghidorah's rule, will you fight your way through every monster to claim the title of King Kong?
