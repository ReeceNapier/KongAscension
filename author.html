About me

Hi, I'm Reece from Northern Ireland, and I am studying Cybersecurity and Forensics in Year 3 at Edinburgh Napier University.

I have been a fan of giant monster movies for a while, particularly Godzilla and King Kong, and with the recently released Godzilla vs Kong, I got a lot of inspiration to develop a text based adventure game with some of the monsters from Legendarys Monsterverse.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing video games, playing football and binging a lot of TV shows and movies, mostly giant monster related or Marvel related.
