Kong Ascension - Prologue

King Ghidorah has taken the throne and is commanding the other monsters to do his bidding, however, Kong, is an alpha, he will not bow to any monster, this angers Ghidorah and he sends his allies after Kong, Kong does not respond well to this and will aim to take the throne from Ghidorah, but first, Kong must defeat Ghidorah’s allies, however, Kong isn’t the only alpha monster challenging for the throne, Godzilla also is challenging for the throne, both of which will eventually showdown, Kong and Godzilla have an ancient rivalry with their ancestors fighting many times, this could be the last time Godzilla and Kong fight against each other, will one of these iconic monsters fall, or can they co-exist?

Press START to begin.
